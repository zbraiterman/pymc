Plots¶

Plots are delegated to the ArviZ. library, a general purpose library for “exploratory analysis of Bayesian models.” For plots, pymc3.<function> are now aliases for ArviZ functions. Thus, the links below will redirect you to ArviZ docs:

  • pymc3.traceplot

  • pymc3.plot_posterior

  • pymc3.forestplot

  • pymc3.compareplot

  • pymc3.autocorrplot

  • pymc3.energyplot

  • pymc3.kdeplot

  • pymc3.densityplot

  • pymc3.pairplot
